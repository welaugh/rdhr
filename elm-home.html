Welcome to ELM Home

In Pakistan overall and especially in region Sindh, students of rural areas face hardship in reaching out to universities due to education standards. Apart from the lack of tangible facilities like building sanitation facilities, outdated syllabus, or computers, government schools are reluctant in training minds for future competition.

Elm Home believes that one intellectual person can change the situation of the entire family. We don't create intellectual but we do our best to transform a student into one by assisting in their passions. Elm Home is an institution in the rural area of Hyderabad city which facilitates students of rural area in studies by providing free and low-cost tuitions, English language and computer classes.

Why support institute tuition?

Growth

More than 5 million primary school-age children are out of school, mostly girls. Lack of schools, high costs of studying and harmful child labor are the main barriers to education in Pakistan.

Safety

Boundary walls, toilets, drinking water and electricity - only 52% of Pakistani school have the basic infrastructure. As of 2017, Pakistan was spending less than 2.8% of its GDP on education, far below the recommended 4 to 6%.

Equality

Especially in rural communities lack of schools, gender discrimination, child labor, child marriage, sexual harassment, insecurity, and attacks on education prevent millions of Pakistani girls from receiving an education.

Opportunity

ELM Learning Home is a safe place where equally boys and girls can develop their skills and passion. We provide low cost or free classes and tuitions with qualified and dedicated teachers.
