Where do we work

Change begins at home and our home is Sindh.

Sindh, Pakistan

Its roots date back to the Indus Valley Civilisation, one of the world's oldest civilizations. Region has it's own language, tradition and culture with over 45 million inhabitants.

Major centre of country's economic activity, the richest in natural resources of petrol, gas and coal. Sindh produces large amount of rice, cotton, mangoes, wheat and sugar cane.

Karachi, the largest city, is country's financial and industrial hub, with the largest airport and largest seaport in Pakistan. The city's an annual GDP is estimated at $114 billions.

Sindh's rich architecture, art, music and poetry are mosaic of human history. The traditional handicrafts of Hala, city nearby Hyderabad, is a synonymus of Sindhi culture.
