Mission

Our mission is to support and strengthen the society at the grass root level by spreading knowledge, providing participatory innovative solutions, generating awareness to end violence against women, facilitating access to resources and services for the poor, marginalized and disadvantaged communities.

What we do

RDHR has completed various projects in different districts of Sindh region with national and international partners.

How do we make a change

Our way to work with the community is very different from conventional NGOs because our relationship with communities is beyond the funded projects. We are part of underprivileged people and they are part of us. This relationship leads our projects towards sustainability.
