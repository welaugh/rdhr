RDHR's programs

GENDER INEQUALITIES

We collect and advocate the gender inequality issues especially domestic violence via the local and national media platform. RDHR helps victims of domestic violence to acquire free legal assistance.

EDUCATION

RDHR helps the student in rural areas with our education wing Elm Home. RDHR also advocates education issues at government level with the help of Sindh Professors and Lecturers' Association (Sipla). RDHR publishes blogs and news on the platform of "Awami Awaz" newspaper to raise issues faced by children and teachers in the region.

ENVIRONMENT

RDHR takes action against climate change by promoting green activities especially planting trees. RDHR also raises awareness about climate change by publishing news about climate change affects on rural communities.

HEALTH

RDHR facilitator Polio Eradication Program in Hyderabad by raising awareness and facilitating polio campaign training and assisting polio workers in the conversion of refusals.

RESEARCH

RDHR currently working on the implementation of a pilot project of a 3-dimensional participatory model to build the capacity of the rural population.
